Sri Janatha Seva Mitra is an NGO in India based in Tirupati (Andhra Pradesh). Established in 2019, we have worked extensively for more than three 3 years to reach out andrehabilitate differently able people from the underprivileged segment.Sri Janatha Seva Mitra works with 4 branches in India while focusing on alleviating disabilities and providing proper Physical, Social, and Economic Rehabilitation to the needy. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive programs in health, education, rehabilitation, corrective surgeries, and helping aid distribution making us the best charity organization in Tirupati.
